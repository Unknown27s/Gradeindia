About GradeCalc India

Built by students, for students. The most comprehensive GPA calculator for Indian universities.

Our Mission

GradeCalc India was created to provide Indian college and university students with a free, accurate, and easy-to-use tool for calculating their academic results. We understand that every university in India has its own grading system, and finding the right calculator can be frustrating.

Our mission is to be the one-stop solution for all GPA-related calculations, regardless of which Indian university you attend.

What We Offer

  • SGPA Calculator - Calculate your Semester Grade Point Average subject-by-subject with accurate grade-to-point mapping for your university.
  • CGPA Calculator - Compute your Cumulative GPA across all completed semesters with credit-weighted calculations.
  • CGPA to Percentage Converter - Convert between CGPA and percentage using your university's official formula.
  • Target CGPA Calculator - Plan ahead by finding out what SGPA you need next semester to reach your academic goals.

Accuracy & Disclaimer

While we strive to provide the most accurate calculations possible based on publicly available information about each university's grading system, we recommend verifying your results with your university's official records. Grading systems and conversion formulas can change, and there may be department-specific variations.

GradeCalc India is a free tool provided "as is" for educational and informational purposes. We are not affiliated with any university mentioned on this website.
