How to Calculate CGPA

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) represents your overall academic performance across all semesters. It is a weighted average of your SGPA values, weighted by the total credits in each semester.

CGPA = Σ(SGPAi × Creditsi) / Σ(Creditsi)

For instance, if Semester 1 has SGPA 8.5 with 24 credits and Semester 2 has SGPA 7.8 with 22 credits, your CGPA would be: (8.5×24 + 7.8×22) / (24+22) = 375.6/46 = 8.17
