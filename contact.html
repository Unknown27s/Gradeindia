Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this calculator accurate?

We use publicly available grade-to-point mappings and CGPA-to-percentage formulas for each university. However, always verify with your university's official documents.

Q: My university is not listed. What should I do?

Use the "Standard 10-Point Scale" or "UGC CBCS" option as a starting point. Then contact us using the form above, and we will add your university's specific grading system.

Q: Is my data safe?

All calculations happen in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your preferences are saved locally on your device only.

Q: Can I use this on my phone?

Yes, GradeCalc India is fully responsive and works great on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.
