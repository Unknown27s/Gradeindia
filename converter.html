About CGPA to Percentage Conversion

Different Indian universities use different formulas to convert CGPA to percentage. The most common formula recommended by UGC is Percentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10. However, many universities have their own specific conversion formulas.

It is important to use the correct formula for your university, especially when applying for higher studies, jobs, or scholarships that require percentage-based marks. Always verify with your university's official documentation.
