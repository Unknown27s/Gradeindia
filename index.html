How to Calculate SGPA

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is calculated by dividing the sum of (grade points × credits) for each subject by the total credits in a semester. The formula is:

SGPA = Σ(Grade Point × Credit) / Σ(Credits)

For example, if you have 5 subjects with credits 4, 4, 3, 3, 2 and grades A+(9), A(8), B+(7), O(10), A(8) respectively, your SGPA would be: (9×4 + 8×4 + 7×3 + 10×3 + 8×2) / (4+4+3+3+2) = 135/16 = 8.44

Supported Universities

Our SGPA calculator supports all major Indian universities including VTU, Anna University, JNTUH, Mumbai University, SPPU (Pune University), AKTU, MAKAUT, GTU, KTU, Calicut University, RGPV, Osmania University, BPUT, and the standard UGC CBCS grading system. Each university has its own grade-to-point mapping and CGPA-to-percentage conversion formula.
