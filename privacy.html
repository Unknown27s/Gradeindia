Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Read our policy to understand how we handle your data.

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

GradeCalc India ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GradeCalc India website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

GradeCalc India is designed with privacy in mind. Here is what we collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or student IDs.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (such as selected university and theme mode). This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.
  • Calculator Inputs: All grades, credits, and CGPA values you enter are processed entirely in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymized data about page views, device type, and general location (country/city level). No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services like Google AdSense and Google Analytics may set cookies on your browser. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal security risk. All calculations are performed client-side (in your browser), and no data is stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for users of all ages, including students under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
